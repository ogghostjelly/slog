GLEACH JALBUM

I really like this jalbum. It incorporates a lot of that messy emo style from casio dad & kara's walk home that I adore. It has a lot of motifs to old gleach songs. overture having an "All the Empathy" motif (I believe? I might be hearing things wrong). kate said obviously having a yoshis island motif. and I believe there was a bedroom community one that I missed. You can really feel the visceral emotion oozing from the jalbum. the beginning of the jalbum was pretty jarring but I like jarring. Favourite track: you are an angel (subject to change though, I never have a favourite track for long)
